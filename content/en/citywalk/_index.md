## The City is the Best Classroom

I'm Wendao, a Citywalk guide.

I believe every street hides a story, and every building has a forgotten history behind it. My Citywalks aren't sightseeing tours — they're expeditions that start with questions. Why was this road built this way? Who owned that building? Where does this place name come from?

**Three cities, endless stories:**
- 🇨🇳 **Shanghai** — Former concessions, shikumen lanes, industrial heritage, urban transformation
- 🇯🇵 **Japan** — Railway corridors, historic districts, urban planning
- 🇺🇸 **San Francisco** — Immigration history, city grid, geography

**Topics covered:** Urban history · Japanese politics & economy · Railways · Geography
